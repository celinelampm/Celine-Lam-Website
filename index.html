About

Celine Lam (b.1999) is a mixed-media artist in NYC. She is an MFA student at Hunter College, with a BFA in Painting from the Rhode Island School of Design. Her current practice focuses on the word Blur (虛). She uses marks and abstract images to depict contemporary phenomena that echo with her generation. Lam often works with mixed-media installations and paintings employing calligraphy practice paper. Lam’s work has been exhibited internationally in New York, Shanghai, Mexico, and Hong Kong.
